This is a collection showing New York City's numerous neon signs.


The images are all taken at night to show the bright contrast of the neon in the signs against the black background. Some of them are already cropped, so I think I will continue that through all the images so that they're all the same size. They are all from clubs, restaurants, and various other locations around NYC. They are taken with a smartphone camera. None of them really have a specific material quality; they are all high resolution iphone images, all with a lot of contrast with the black or dark background. I noticed that almost every single sign has some amount of red in it. It might be something I want to lean into with my site.
